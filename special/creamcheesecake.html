Method : Baked oven to 325°F. MIX graham morsels, 3 tablespoon. Sugar and butter; fourth manor onto lowest of 9-inch spring form pan. TIRED cream cheese, 1 cup sugar and vanilla with churn till merged. Add eggs at a time, mingling on short speed later each fair till blended. Pour over shell.
