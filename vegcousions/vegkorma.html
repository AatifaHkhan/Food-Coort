Vegetable korma / kurma very healthy and testy food and served with rotis, chapatti, parotta or even pooris. There are different types of recipes are used in hotels. This website give a simple ways to cook veg korma healthy and testy.
     
 

Ingredients: Vegetables - 1 and 1/4 cup(I used a combination of carrots, gobi, peas ,potato and beans you also add other vegetables as per your Requirement or your choice.) Onion - 1 medium sized, Tomato - 1 medium sized, Ginger Garlic paste  - 2 tsp, Garam masala powder - 1 tsp, Red Chilli powder - 2 tablespon, Coriander powder - 1 tsp, Lemon juice - 2 tsp (or) Thick Curd - 1 tablespon, Coriander leaves - 1/2 tablespon, Salt - to taste, Fresh grated coconut - 1/2 , cup, Cashews – 4, Poppy seeds - 1/2 tsp, Fennel seeds - 1 tsp, Split roasted gram - 2 tsp, Green Chillies – 2, Cloves – 2, Cinnamon - 1/2 inch piece, Cardamom – 1, Oil - 1 tablespon, Maze(Japthri) – 1, Bayleaf – 1, Curry leaves – few.

Method: Start with grind the materials listed under 'to grind' with tiny water to a well paste, Set apart. Chop the root vegetable and clean it well. Parboil the root vegetable just half-baked along with little salt, set apart. Heat oil in a kadai, and add the things under 'to temper' then add ginger garlic paste followed by onion saute well until slightly browned. Then add chopped tomatoes stir fry until syrupy and raw fragrance leaves. Then add red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder and fry for 2 mins for the masala powders to mixture well. Then add the coconut paste, 3/4 to 1 cup water and allow it to boil for 5mins in low flame until raw smell leaves. Then add the veggies, required salt and simmer. Simmer and allow it to boil till you get a thick kurma consistency. If you are adding curd add it at this stage. Add chopped coriander leaves and lemon juice.
