Potato Kebab Recipe is very different to other kebabs recipe because is pure vegetarian kebab because in this recipe all ingredients. This Potato Kebab is very easy and healthy.
     
 

Ingredients : kernel corn 2 cups, Boiled potato 1/2 cup and grated , cheese cheddar grated 2 tablespoon , green chilli  2 chopped , ginger 2 teaspoon chopped, black pepper powder 1/2 teaspoon, garam masala 1/2 teaspoon, white pepper powder 1/2 teaspoon, mace powder 1 pinch , leaves Few fresh mint, Salt as per required , refined oil 3-4 teaspoon.

Method : Vent the mush, put all the materials to it and combination well. Distribute the fusion into equivalent rations and make butt shaped kebabs. Warmth oil in a surfaced pan and narrow fry the kebabs over average heat till crisp golden color. Present with green chutney.
